About Our Community

Welcome to GaTech Indian, the central hub for Indian students enrolled in Georgia Tech's prestigious Online Masters programs: OMSCS, OMSA, and OM Cyber Security. Our community thrives on collaboration and support, connecting students across multiple platforms to share knowledge, experiences, and opportunities.

Whether you're just starting your application journey or are a seasoned student looking to help others, our community provides a supportive environment where you can find answers, make connections, and build lasting friendships with fellow Indian students.

Frequently Asked Questions

This community serves as a platform for Indian students in GaTech's online programs to connect, network, share advice on coursework, discuss career opportunities, and organize local meetups. We help each other navigate the challenges of online learning and career growth.
